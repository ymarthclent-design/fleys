About Us

Fleyi is a creative handmade craft shop dedicated to beautifully crafted felt and clay creations. We specialize in unique, handcrafted pieces made with care, passion, and attention to detail. Our products are more than just decorations, they are expressions of creativity, warmth, and personality that add charm to any space or occasion. Every piece is thoughtfully designed and carefully handmade, making each item special and one of a kind.

WHAT WE OFFER

◦ Handmade Felt Crafts – Cute keychains, ornaments, plush figures, and personalized designs.

◦ Clay Crafts – Miniature figures, charms, accessories, and custom-made keepsakes.

◦ Custom Orders – Personalized crafts for birthdays, gifts, souvenirs, and special events.
